# Core Features

Ginnie AI brings a suite of powerful, autonomous tools directly into your daily workflow. Here is a breakdown of the core functionalities included in the platform.

## 1. Inbox AI

Ginnie AI categorizes your emails in the background so you only see what matters.

- **Smart Triage**: Evaluates incoming emails and categorizes them automatically.
- **Contextual Summaries**: Read 2-3 sentence summaries instead of long, verbose threads.
- **Draft Generation**: Instantly generate professional, context-aware replies that match your specific tone of voice.

## 2. Calendar AI

An autonomous scheduling assistant that handles your time.

- Parses incoming meeting requests from email threads.
- Automatically schedules meetings into Google Calendar/Outlook.
- Strictly respects designated "Deep Work" blocks to prevent meeting fatigue.

## 3. Visual Workflow Automations

A beautiful node-based automation builder.

- Visually drag and drop nodes to create triggers and actions.
- Example: *WHEN email arrives* → *If subject contains "Invoice"* → *ACTION: Save to Google Drive*.
- Provides real-time visual feedback with animated SVG connection lines.

## 4. Smart Search

Semantic, conversational retrieval across all your connected apps.

- Ask natural language questions (e.g., "What did Sarah say about the Q3 budget?").
- The AI searches across Gmail, Drive, and Slack simultaneously to retrieve the exact context.

## 5. Razorpay Subscriptions

Ginnie AI includes a fully functional payment and subscription flow.

- **Free Tier**: Capped usage for basic testing.
- **Pro & Elite Tiers**: Upgrading via Razorpay unlocks higher API rate limits and faster execution speeds.
- Secure webhook verification instantly applies upgrades upon successful checkout.
